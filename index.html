I am from Moscow, Russia, and I moved to Finland in August 2016 to study for my Bachelor's degree in Information Technology in Oulu University of Applied Sciences. The studies mostly concentrated on the Web, Application and Internet Sevices Development, but besides that I had an opportunity to explore other areas of Computer Science, such as its basics, hardware, operating systems, security and other.

After the second year of studies I started working and have been continuing ever since. Fall semester 2018 I spent on exchange in Politecnico di Torino, Turin, Italy, after that on return to Oulu I started a new job. I graduated from OAMK in December 2019.

In October 2020 I moved to Helsinki to study for Master's degree in Computer, Commutication and Information Sciences in Aalto University. My track is Big Data and Large-Scale Computing and I am concentrating my studies around AI, ML and Data-related subjects.
