Hello!

My name is Mariia. I am from Moscow, Russia. I moved to Finland in August 2016 to study for my Bachelor's degree in Information Technology at Oulu University of Applied Sciences. The studies concentrated on Web, Application, and Internet Sevices Development, but besides that, I had an opportunity to explore other areas of Computer Science, such as its core, hardware, operating systems, security, and others. Fall semester 2018 I spent on exchange in Politecnico di Torino, Turin, Italy.

In October 2020 I moved to Helsinki to study for a Master's degree in Computer, Communication and Information Sciences at Aalto University. My track is Big Data and Large-Scale Computing and I am concentrating my studies around AI, ML, and Data-related subjects. I have also been employed by the department for several Teacher Assistant jobs.

I started working in 2018 and since then I've tried myself in various areas of IT, such as DevOps, Blockchain, Machine Learning, and Artificial Intelligence in different companies and projects. I am passionate about Data Science and I enjoy statistics and math behind it.

Please feel free to contact me and/or explore my social media to learn more about my experience and qualifications!
